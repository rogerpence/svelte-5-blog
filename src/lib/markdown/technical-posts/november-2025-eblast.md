  The following is the email text for new marketing campaign. The email announces a webcast that covers ways our business can help them ensure a future for their critical application. 

Please suggest subject lines for this email

The email text is: 
RPG's dilemma

Your IBM i RPG applications are critically important to your business's success. They manage your corporate data, your unique workflows, and critical processes; all of which provide the backbone of your business.
These applications have served your business well for decades. However, they were designed decades ago and in many cases lack the modern features, interoperability, and the efficiency your business demands for the future.

Your challenge

Your challenge is determining how to persist your RPG applications so that they can continue to help ensure your business continues to deliver its unique value to your customers and partners.
The clock is ticking on the availability of RPG programming talent. Can your business continue with your RPG programmers?
ASNA has the talent and tools to help ensure a future for your IBM i applications and your business.


What we'll cover

•	How RPG programmer retirement threatens your business
•	Putting your RPG application in younger programmer hands
•	A rational step-wise RPG modernization path with ASNA Wings and ASNA Monarch
•	IBM i DB2 or SQL Server — your choice
•	The many ways ASNA's Services Team can help you with your IBM i and .NET projects
